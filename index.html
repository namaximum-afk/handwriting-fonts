Frequently asked questions about handwriting fonts

What are handwriting fonts used for?

Handwriting fonts are digital typefaces that imitate real handwritten letters. Designers use them for logos, quotes, social media posts, wedding invitations, planners and any project where a human, personal touch is important.

Can I use these handwriting fonts for commercial projects?

Yes. With the Creative Fabrica subscription you get commercial use rights for the handwriting fonts in this collection, so you can sell designs on marketplaces or work with clients without buying extra individual licenses.

Are handwriting fonts good for Cricut and print on demand?

Many of the handwriting fonts featured here are optimized for cutting machines like Cricut and for print on demand products. They have clean curves, complete character sets and are tested on T‑shirts, mugs, posters and other popular POD items.
